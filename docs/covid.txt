COVID-19 is caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. Common symptoms: fever, cough, shortness of breath, fatigue, loss of taste/smell. 

Prevention: wear masks, wash hands, social distancing, vaccination. 

Mild cases: rest, hydration, over-the-counter meds. Severe cases: seek medical attention immediately. 

Emergency signs: difficulty breathing, chest pain, confusion, bluish lips/face.
